ABOUT

The best way to get to know someone is to have a discussion with them, a dialogue. Only through talking to someone can we connect with them on a human level and break down misconceptions and stereotypes created by society. Islam in Sydney tells the experiences of Muslims living in Sydney. It is a platform for you to hear their stories and learn about a minority group in our community. Fear is used to mobilise people and justify actions. Question your media and what you are told about groups of people, and open your mind to how and why people are being isolated in our society.
